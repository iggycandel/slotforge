⚡Game Features
What happens when you toggle a feature?
Each enabled feature unlocks three things at once:
  1. A dedicated screen group appears in the Flow workspace (intro / in-round / outro tabs as needed)
  2. A configuration card appears below this list, where you set the feature's parameters
  3. The feature is added to the generated GDD, math spec, and asset deliverables
Mechanics & behaviour are configured in the Features top-bar workspace — this list is just the master switch.

◆Symbol Set
What are these?
These are the icons that spin on your reels and pay out. They group into three families:
  • High symbols — top-paying themed icons (cowboy hat, sheriff star…). Lower frequency, bigger payouts.
  • Low symbols — fillers (A · K · Q · J · 10 · 9). Higher frequency, smaller payouts.
  • Special symbols — Wild, Scatter, Bonus etc. Trigger features and substitute for paying symbols.
Set the count below, click Apply Symbol Set, then design each symbol in the Art workspace.
